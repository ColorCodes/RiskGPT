Frontier Odds

A rogue-lite dice conquest for one human commander vs. three scheming rivals.

Tile Traits

  • Fortified Ridge: +1 defense for defender.
  • Battle Shrine: +1 attack for the tile's owner.
  • Haunted Ruin: -1 attack for whoever attacks from it.
  • Gold Mine: +2 gold income.

Click one of your tiles, then click an adjacent enemy tile to attack. End your turn to let the rival AIs plot.
